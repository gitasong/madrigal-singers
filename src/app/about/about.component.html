The Portland Madrigal Singers was founded in 2017 by Nicole Freed. Co-directed by Nicole Freed and Lennie Cottrell, the Portland Madrigal Singers aims to give a high-quality musical experience to talented amateurs. Rehearsals are ongoing; we audition new singers every fall, and we offer a variety of concerts and flash mobs around the city and surrounding communities. Contact us at 503.xxx.xxxx for more details.
